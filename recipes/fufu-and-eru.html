fufu and eru

fufu and eru is vegetable soup made up of finely shredded leaves of the eru or okok.

The eru is stewed with waterleaf or spinach, palm oil, crayfish, and either smoked fish, cow skin (kanda) or beef. This dish is traditionally eaten with fermented water-fufu or garri.

Ingrendients

  • eru leaf
  • waterleaf
  • crewfish
  • smoked or fresh meat
  • kanda
  • smoked fish
  • palm oil

    1. fix and cute meat and fish
    2. select the crew fish and grain
    3. boil the meat and fish
    4. add washed eru and waterleaf to the boiled meat
    5. put seat and maggi in the previous process
    6. Add oil to the eru
    7. Then put you crewfish last

      NOTE: The reason while you put the crewfish last is to make sure that the crewfish taste really well by the time the whole process is over

    8. Then stell make sure all the ingredients are well mixed
    9. Then make the fufu/garri by puting warm water on the fire
    10. Then put the fufu/garri in to the boiling water
    11. Stell until its ready
